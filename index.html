Got Questions?

What is professional drifting?

Professional drifting is a motorsport where drivers intentionally oversteer, while maintaining control, through turns. It's a test of skill, precision, and car control at high speeds.

What cars are best for drifting?

While many cars can drift, some legends like the Nissan GTR, Toyota Supra, BMW M4, and Ford Mustang are iconic for their power and handling, making them drift favorites.

How can I join a drift event?

Joining a drift event is straightforward. Check our events page for schedules and registration details. We welcome drivers of all skill levels.

What are the risks involved?

Drifting, like any motorsport, has risks. We prioritize safety with professional track marshals and strict safety protocols to minimize dangers.

Can beginners start drifting?

Absolutely! We offer introductory drift sessions and training programs designed for newcomers to learn the basics in a safe, controlled environment.
